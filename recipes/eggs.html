Scrambled Eggs

Description

Eggs are protein rich. Here is a quick, easy recipe to start the day: Scrambled eggs with a chopped tomatoe.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 chopped tomatoe

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a cast iron skillet
  2. Add the chopped tomatoe and let it heat for about 3-5 minutes
  3. Add the cracked eggs then stir slowly across the skillet
  4. Add salt, pepper and cumin to your liking
